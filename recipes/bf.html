Banana Fosters

Description

Check out these fucking pancakes. Have you ever seen such delectable shit? I mean seriously. God would fuck Satan for these pancakes. You won't believe your eyes.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 3 1/2 tablespoons rum
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 bananas, peeled and slived lenghthwise and crosswise
  • 1/4 cup coarsely chopped walnuts
  • 1 pint vanilla ice cream
